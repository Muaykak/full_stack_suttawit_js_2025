Riko (リコ) is a 12-year-old orphan from the town at the edge of The Abyss, Orth, and the main protagonist of Made in Abyss.[1] She is the only child of the White Whistle Lyza "The Annihilator". When she was only two, her mother began her Last Dive to the bottom of The Abyss. Since her father, Torka, had died before she was even born, she was left in the care of Belchero Orphanage.[2] Her dream is to become a White Whistle, a legendary type of Delver, and explore the depths of The Abyss with her group in order to learn its many secrets and to find her mother Faputa (ファプタ) is the final child of Irumyuui and is referred to as "Princess of the Narehate" by the inhabitants of The Village of Iruburu within the 6th Layer of The Abyss.[1] She is described by Majikaja as "the embodiment of Value", and is highly regarded by the Narehate of the village.[2] While the citizens of the village are unable to leave it, conversely, Faputa is unable to enter.[3][4] She can speak both the language of Iruburu and the language of the Delvers.[5][6] She is currently attached to Riko's delving squad, Hello Abyss. Nanachi (ナナチ) is a Narehate; a person who survived the 6th Layer's Curse and was left deformed as a result, but, unlike most other Narehate, still retained a sound mind.[1] Possessing vast knowledge of The Abyss' ecosystem, vegetation, and even the use of medicines, Nanachi is currently a member of Hello Abyss, and the tritagonist of Made in Abyss. Their gender is ambiguous.[2]
